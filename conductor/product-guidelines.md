# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
Casual & Friendly: Our content should feel like a supportive peer. Keep it simple, minimize academic jargon, and prioritize clarity.

## Visual Branding: Cyberpunk/Dark
The simulators should use a vibrant, dark-themed UI.
- **Color Palette:** High-contrast, neon-on-dark (e.g., deep purples/blues, bright accent colors for simulation states).
- **Vibe:** Tech-focused, energetic, and engaging.

## UX Principles
- **Mobile-Responsive:** The tools must perform seamlessly across all screen sizes.
- **Instant Feedback:** Interactions must have immediate, low-latency visual responses to maintain engagement.
